Game rules

  1. Only push the previous defense tower can push the next defense tower, when pushing the enemy defense tower should pay attention to the soldier is not under the tower, avoid the defense tower to their maximum damage value to reduce their own health.
  2. When there are no minions in the enemy tower, heroes entering the enemy tower will be preferentially attacked by the tower. In the case of minions, the tower will attack the marked minions first, then you can flatten the tower, gradually reducing the damage of the tower.
  3. When you kill all buffs, overlords and tyrants, only the last person you kill can trigger the effects, and only the last person who deals damage and experience can trigger the effects.
